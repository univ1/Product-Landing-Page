Phem-Bot: Collect, Build, Play

Features

Our engineers work hard to ensure that every aspect of Phem-Bot is made using state of the art technology and industry-leading resources.

Collect every piece to give your Phem-Bot the most functionality. Show the world what you're made of!

Phem-Bot's games are crowd-sourced and thus contribute to its endless replayability. There are over two million curated games you can play with your Phem-Bot, and more that are being made everyday!

Voted #1 Toy of the Year by Awesome Toy World, Toy Lovers Unite, and Baby Got Toys.
